I'm a data journalist and reporter at Reach Plc, producing breaking news and data-driven investigations using public records, analysis, and visualization. Before moving to New York, I worked as a freelance journalist in Pakistan. My work has appeared in The Guardian, New Lines Magazine, The New Humanitarian, and The Diplomat. I cover human rights, climate, immigration, and Balochistan.
